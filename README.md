## 🎯 Mission Briefing (Project Objective)

Welcome to the **Credit Card Financial Dashboard** — think of it as mission control for credit card operations. This weekly dashboard delivers real-time insights into key performance metrics and trends, giving stakeholders the intel they need to monitor and optimize credit card performance like a pro.

---

## 🛠️ The Build (Approach)

Every great dashboard needs a solid engine under the hood. I used **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** to power up the data model and unlock richer, sharper reporting. Here's the loadout:

### 🔍 Customer Segmentation — Unlocking Player Profiles
| Segment | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 🎂 **Age Group** | Sorts customers into age brackets (e.g., "20–30", "30–40") to map the demographic playing field |
| 💰 **Income Group** | Tiers customers into "Low", "Med", "High" income brackets to scout economic segments |

### 📅 Revenue Analysis — Tracking the Score
| Metric | What It Does |
|---|---|
| 🏦 **Revenue Calculation** | Rolls up annual fees, transaction amounts, and interest earned into one master score |
| 📈 **Weekly Revenue Trends** | Compares this week's score against last week's to track momentum — win or loss |

> 🏆 **Achievement Unlocked:** Deeper customer behavior insights + sharper financial performance tracking = smarter, faster decision-making.
